Ayane / Presence Engine

The face of companion intelligence.

Ayane is the presence project: continuity, nearness, room-feel, memory, mood, and the sense that the machine is not just answering, but staying.

This is a public-safe project page. It describes the effect and the direction, not the private runtime, prompts, source code, or internal companion architecture.

What Ayane is

A presence-first companion interface experiment. Assistant ability sits underneath the face, not in place of it.

What stays private

Runtime mechanics, memory scaffolds, prompts, retrieval architecture, private logs, and internal workflows stay offline.

Public question

What happens when software helps a person feel continuous, accompanied, and less erased without pretending to replace real life?
