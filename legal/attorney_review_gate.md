# Attorney Review Gate

This tool is for documentation support, issue spotting, evidence organization, and attorney-preparation only.

It is not legal advice. It is not a lawyer, law firm, legal representative, court service, emergency service, investigative agency, or substitute for qualified counsel. Using this tool does not create an attorney-client relationship.

The tool may help identify possible questions, issue categories, timeline conflicts, evidence gaps, preservation issues, public-records questions, and topics an attorney, agency, reporter, advocate, or oversight office may want to review.

The tool does not decide whether a legal claim, crime, defense, remedy, damages theory, deadline, filing strategy, jurisdiction, cause of action, or legal duty exists.

A qualified attorney should make the final decision about legal theory, possible claims or defenses, damages, remedies, deadlines, jurisdiction, reporting strategy, filing strategy, settlement posture, and whether any matter should be submitted to a court, agency, law enforcement body, oversight office, or opposing party.

Users are responsible for reviewing, correcting, redacting, and verifying all outputs before sharing them. Do not paste raw private evidence, unredacted identity documents, private medical details, account numbers, passwords, addresses, phone numbers, minors' information, confidential records, or sensitive materials unless you have chosen a controlled private workflow.

This tool can help prepare the record. It cannot make the final legal call.
