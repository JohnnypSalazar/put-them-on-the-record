## What this is

This is a public-safe record and product layer.

It helps turn scattered facts, dates, receipts, messages, filings, records, and open questions into readable packets, timelines, summaries, and attorney-review topics.

It is built for people who need the record to stop slipping through their fingers.

## What this is not

This is not legal advice.  
This is not a law firm.  
This is not a substitute for qualified counsel.  
This is not a raw evidence warehouse.  
This is not the private engine.  

The public gets the clean front door.  
The private engine stays offline.

## Attorney Review Gate

The free packet builder is for documentation support, issue spotting, evidence organization, and attorney preparation.

It may help identify possible questions, evidence gaps, timeline conflicts, preservation topics, public-records questions, and attorney-review categories.

It does not decide legal claims, crimes, damages, deadlines, remedies, jurisdiction, filing strategy, or legal theory.

A qualified attorney should make the final call.

## Public-safe rule

No raw private evidence.  
No unredacted private identifiers.  
No source code.  
No private workflows.  
No internal engine details.  
No private companion architecture.  

Readable public signal first.  
Private machinery stays protected.
